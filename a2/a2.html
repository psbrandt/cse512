Dataset

The dataset used for this analysis is the SyntheticMass patient population. This dataset contains the electronic health record data from "realistic by fictional" residents of the state of Massachusetts. The data were generated by MITRE Corporation, which is a not-for-profile company that operates multiple Federally Funded Research and Development Centers (FFRDCs).

Due to the sensitive nature of electronic health record data, there are essentially no public datasets available. The few datasets that are available are either not free, or have been so heavily anonymized that their analytic value has been substantially decreased. Although I do have access to EHR data for my research, without IRB approval I can't share analyses, so I've chosen the SyntheticMass dataset to practice exporatory data analysis for this assignment.

The dataset is available online at: syntheticmass.mitre.org, and the full description of how the data were generated can be found in the article published on the subject: 10.1093/jamia/ocx147.
